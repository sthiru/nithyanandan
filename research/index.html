Research

Our broad research interests are in the fields of Ultrafast Optics, Lasers and Photonics. We undertake both fundamental and applied level research by developing advanced theoretical and computational models, followed by finest experiments. A recent development in our activities are the application of machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) in photonic system, aiming at adding new functionalities and to improve performance. Our group also focuses on quantum technologies like quantum computing, quantum machine learning etc.,
